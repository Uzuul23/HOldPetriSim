Clipboard

HPSim uses a proprietary format to exchange data from one window to another through the clipboard. This format can not be interpreted by other applications. Consequently, HPSim accepts only insertion from the clipboard, when they are in the known format.

All currently marked objects will be copied to the clipboard, when selecting the menu entry “ArrangeàCopy”.

All currently marked objects will be copied to the clipboard and deleted in the current window, when selecting the menu entry “ArrangeàCut”.

All currently marked objects will be inserted from the clipboard, when selecting the menu entry “ArrangeàPaste”.

An arc will only be copied into the clipboard, when the objects to both ends of the arc were selected as well. Labels will be always be copied into the clipboard along with their objects (owners), even though the were not selected.
